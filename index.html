2013

Realizations

  • I love new york
  • Being honest is more important than being happy
  • I would rather do many needful works than a few great works
  • A job doesn’t have to be my passion or main life force
  • Interdependence is the answer to emptiness.
  • There is no self
  • Everyone is performing, all of the time
  • There is no limit to the number of ways of doing things
  • You have to breathe out to breathe in

Resolutions

  • Apologize less
  • Learn to challenge others compassionately
  • Be more creative
  • Learn to examine uncomfortable feelings, to embrace them and talk about them
  • Be more proactive and less reactive
  • Learn to detach/not get too attached/not make things too weighty
  • Do what is joyful, it doesn’t matter if you are good at it or not
  • Slow down, be more mindful and intentional
  • Be more vulnerable*
  • Get better at reading critically

*if you are wondering why i put all of this online, i see it as an excercise in vulnerability, and a way to share things that are meaningful to me with friends. if you want to problematize that or ask me questions about it i'm happy to talk.
